About Us

We are the world's largest university

A college is an educational institution that offers a variety of programs, including undergraduate degrees, vocational education, and more. Colleges can be part of a larger institution or stand alone, and they can be private or public. In the United States, the term "college" can also refer to a university's undergraduate program, a residential college, or a community college.

Here are some things to know about college:

Education


College can provide a strong base for a student's career, and can help them develop in-depth knowledge in their chosen field.

Skills


College can help students develop personal and professional skills, such as effective communication and time management. Extracurricular activities can help students develop leadership and teamwork skills.

Cost


College tuition and fees vary from school to school. In the 2023-2024 school year, the average cost of tuition and fees was $42,162 for private colleges, $23,630 for out-of-state students at public universities, and $10,662 for in-state residents at public schools.

Environment


Many colleges are environmentally progressive, with water fountains that have water bottle refill buttons and recycle bins. Some schools are even experimenting with new eco-technologies.
